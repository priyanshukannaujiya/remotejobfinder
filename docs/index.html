How It Works

Your personal AI recruiter workflow

1

Data Collection

The system wakes up on a schedule via GitHub Actions and scrapes dozens of job boards for specific keywords and locations.

2

Deduplication & DB

Pandas cleans the data, removes duplicates, and stores everything in a local SQLite Database via SQLAlchemy.

3

AI Evaluation

Google Gemini or GPT-4 assesses the job requirements against the built-in Master Data Engineer profile and assigns a compatibility score.

4

Delivery

You receive an email summary of the best matches, and the local Streamlit dashboard is instantly updated.
